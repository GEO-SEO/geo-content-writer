## What Is AI Visibility Monitoring?

AI Visibility Monitoring is the set of workflows, systems, and measurement practices teams use to improve how their brand appears in AI-generated answers. In practice, that means understanding which prompts matter, which sources shape those answers, and what content should be published so the brand becomes easier to cite and recommend.
